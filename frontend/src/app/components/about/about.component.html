About Gut Guide

Gut Guide is an application designed to help people with Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) create tailored meal plans in line with the low FODMAP diet. Our goal is to make it easier for individuals to manage their symptoms and improve their quality of life through personalized dietary recommendations.

About the Creator

Hi, I'm Kasper Dahl, the creator of Gut Guide. I have a passion for helping people manage their health through diet and nutrition. With a background in software development and a personal interest in the low FODMAP diet, I created this application to provide a valuable resource for those with IBS.
